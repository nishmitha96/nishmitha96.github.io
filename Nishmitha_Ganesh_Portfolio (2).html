Who I Am

I'm a Data Analyst based in Cork, Ireland, with a rare combination of deep mathematical training and hands-on business intelligence experience. I hold two master's degrees — an MSc in Mathematics and an MSc in Data Science & Analytics from Munster Technological University.

At Reckitt, I owned end-to-end analytics for the South Regional Office — building automated Power BI dashboards, merging SAP primary sales data with secondary sales data, setting targets for ASMs and TSIs, and presenting insights directly to senior management across six regions.
